Mechanical Engineering



About Department:

The Mechanical Engineering Department, led by Prof. Sanjay Patel, is known for its commitment to excellence and innovation. Our experienced faculty members, under his guidance, strive to provide quality education and conduct cutting-edge research programs. We focus on practical learning through industrial visits and hands-on training, preparing our students for the challenges of the engineering world. Our alumni, who occupy esteemed positions in various industries, are a testament to the department's success in nurturing talent and fostering a spirit of innovation.


Salient features of Department:

Established in 2010, the Mechanical Engineering Department has consistently produced skilled technocrats and leaders in the field. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in mechanical engineering principles and practical skills. We offer state-of-the-art facilities, including CAD software and advanced testing equipment, to enhance students' learning experience. The department also encourages research and collaboration with industries to address real-world challenges.
