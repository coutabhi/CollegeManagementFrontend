Computer Engineering



About Department:

The Computer Engineering Department, led by Dr. Rajesh Sharma, is committed to excellence in education and research in the field of computer engineering. Our dynamic faculty members, under his leadership, focus on providing students with a strong foundation in computer science principles, combined with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies. We prepare our graduates to become leaders in the rapidly evolving field of computer engineering.


Salient features of Department:

Established in 2012, the Computer Engineering Department offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of topics, including software development, hardware design, networking, and cybersecurity. Our state-of-the-art labs and research facilities provide students with opportunities to explore emerging technologies and engage in innovative projects. The department also emphasizes industry collaboration and encourages students to participate in internships and co-op programs to gain practical experience.
